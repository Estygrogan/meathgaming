Gaming Community and Events

Why Join Meath Gaming?

Games for All

Whether it is Call of Duty or Stardew Valley, if a device runs it we probably play it! You are sure to find a fellow gamer with similar interests!

Events, tournaments and LAN Parties

We hold regular events, ranging from Halo 2 LAN parties to League of Legends tournaments. We occasionally have prize pools for our bigger events too! We often organise charity events as well, supporting good causes worldwide!

Access to Gaming Devices

Everyone knows how hard (and expensive) it is to get access to consoles or pcs. At our events, we have several Playstation and Xbox consoles, as well as a small number of gaming PCs, allowing you to take your gaming to the next level.
